# The Fine-Structure Constant (α)

> *"I am the Alpha and the Omega..."* — Revelation 1:8

## The Constant

*   **Symbol:** α (alpha)
*   **Definition:** The coupling constant governing the strength of the electromagnetic interaction between elementary charged particles.
*   **CODATA 2018 Value:** α = 1 / 137.035999084(21)  
    ≈ **0.0072973525693(11)**
*   **Significance:** A dimensionless, fundamental constant of nature. It defines the "fineness" of the structure of matter.
