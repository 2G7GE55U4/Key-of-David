# Matthew 21:44 & Luke 20:18: The Stone of Stumbling
> *"And whosoever shall fall on this stone shall be broken: but on whomsoever it shall fall, it will grind him to powder."* — Matthew 21:44 (KJV)
> *"Whosoever shall fall upon that stone shall be broken; but on whomsoever it shall fall, it will grind him to powder."* — Luke 20:18 (KJV)

## The Prophetic Claim
Jesus Christ identifies Himself as **the stone** (λίθος) that the builders rejected, which becomes the **head of the corner** (κεφαλὴ γωνίας). This stone is both a foundation for believers and a stone of stumbling and rock of offense for unbelievers (Isaiah 8:14, 1 Peter 2:6-8). The act of **falling upon the stone** (voluntary brokenness) leads to salvation; being **crushed by the stone** (judicial grinding) leads to destruction.
