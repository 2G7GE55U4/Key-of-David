# Matthew 3:16-17 – The Baptism and Divine Voice

> *"And Jesus, when he was baptized, went up straightway out of the water: and, lo, the heavens were opened unto him, and he saw the Spirit of God descending like a dove, and lighting upon him: And lo a voice from heaven, saying, This is my beloved Son, in whom I am well pleased."* — Matthew 3:16-17 (KJV)

## The Prophetic Claim
The baptism of Jesus marks the **inauguration of His public ministry** with a triune manifestation:
1. **The Son**: Jesus being baptized in the Jordan.
2. **The Spirit**: Descending "like a dove" upon Him.
3. **The Father**: Speaking from heaven, "This is my beloved Son."

This event fulfills Isaiah 42:1 ("Behold my servant... I have put my spirit upon him") and Psalm 2:7 ("Thou art my Son; this day have I begotten thee"). It is the **first public revelation of the Trinity** in the New Testament.
