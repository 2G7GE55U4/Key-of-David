# Revelation 11:11 – The Spirit of Life from God

“And after three days and an half the Spirit of life from God entered into them, and they stood upon their feet; and great fear fell upon them which saw them.” — Revelation 11:11 (KJV)[reference:0]

## The Prophetic Claim
This verse describes the resurrection of the two witnesses. After lying dead for exactly 3.5 days, the “Spirit of life” (πνεῦμα ζωῆς) from God enters them, and they stand up, causing great terror. The event is a powerful sign of God’s authority over death and a foreshadowing of the final resurrection. The phrase “Spirit of life” directly ties the event to the divine source of all life.
