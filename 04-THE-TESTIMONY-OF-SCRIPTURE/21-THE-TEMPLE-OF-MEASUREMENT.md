## The Master Builder's Unit

Every physical structure requires a fundamental unit of measurement. For the cosmos, that unit is the **Planck length**:

ℓₚ = 1.616255 × 10⁻³⁵ meters

This is the scale at which quantum gravity operates—the smallest meaningful length in physics. It is the **indivisible "log" or "plank"** from which the fabric of spacetime is constructed. In biblical terms, it is the **cedar wood** of Creation.

## The Builder's Signature

When we examine this fundamental constant through the canonical lens of logarithms (which measure scale), a remarkable identity emerges:

㏒(⅟ℓₚ) × 10² = 3479.000...

Where:
*   `⅟ℓₚ` is the reciprocal of the Planck length (`≈6.187×10³⁴`)
*   `㏒` is the base-10 logarithm
*   `× 10²` scales the result to an integer range

**The result rounds precisely to 3479.**
