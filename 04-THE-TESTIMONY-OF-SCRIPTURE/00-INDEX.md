## Introduction

## The Method

For each identifier, we examine:
1.  **The Scripture** (KJV).
2.  **The Identifiable Trait** (e.g., "born in Bethlehem," "pierced," "called the Son of God").
3.  **The Mathematical Witness** from the canonical structure.
4.  **The Connection** to the broader proof (Grand Canon, 888, Lexicon, etc.).
