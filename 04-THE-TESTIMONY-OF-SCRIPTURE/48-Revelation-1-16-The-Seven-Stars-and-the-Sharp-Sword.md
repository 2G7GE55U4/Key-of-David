# Revelation 1:16: The Seven Stars, the Sharp Sword, and the Solar Countenance

> *"And he had in his right hand seven stars: and out of his mouth went a sharp twoedged sword: and his countenance was as the sun shineth in his strength."* — Revelation 1:16 (KJV)

## 1. The Scriptural Identifier
The vision of the Son of Man (Rev 1:13) continues with three definitive attributes:
1.  **Sovereign Authority:** He holds **seven stars** (later identified as "the angels of the seven churches," Rev 1:20) in His **right hand**.
2.  **Judgmental Word:** From His mouth proceeds a **sharp two-edged sword**.
3.  **Divine Glory:** His **countenance** shines **as the sun in its strength**.
This is a complete portrait of Christ's authority over the church, His power to judge by His word, and His radiant divine nature.
