# John 10:11 – The Good Shepherd

> *"I am the good shepherd: the good shepherd giveth his life for the sheep."* — John 10:11 (KJV)

## The Prophetic Claim
In this profound "I AM" statement, Jesus identifies Himself as the **Good Shepherd** who:
1. **Owns the sheep** – "My sheep hear my voice..." (John 10:27)
2. **Protects the sheep** – "...the good shepherd giveth his life for the sheep"
3. **Knows His sheep intimately** – "I know my sheep, and am known of mine" (John 10:14)

This declaration fulfills Old Testament prophecies of God as Shepherd (Psalm 23:1, Isaiah 40:11) and messianic promises (Ezekiel 34:23). The "good shepherd" contrasts with hired hands who flee when danger comes, emphasizing Christ's **sacrificial love**.
