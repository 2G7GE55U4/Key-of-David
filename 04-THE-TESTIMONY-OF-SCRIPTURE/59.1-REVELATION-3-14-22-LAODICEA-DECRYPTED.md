1.  **The Speaker's Final Authority:** Identified as "the Amen" and "the beginning of the creation of God," His identity is linked to the alpha (G1) and the foundational curse-bearing mountain for the soul (H5858, H543).

Its self-declared wealth is contrasted with the true constant of growth (`e`), and its blindness (H5786) is timestamped to the Hebrew year 5786 AM (2025/26 CE).
3.  **The Counsel of Constants:** The command to buy "gold tried in the fire" resolves to the mathematical constant **`e` (2.718...)**, the base of natural growth and true profit. The white raiment and eyesalve are derived from Christ's own number (G2424) and point to the Lord God (G2316).
4.  **The Door and the Throne:** The promise "I stand at the door" is a direct arithmetic transform of "Behold" (G2400 → G2374). The ultimate promise to the overcomer—the Throne (G2362)—is constructed from the canonical structure itself (`1189` chapters) and a fundamental growth operation (`[1+1/3]^27`).
5.  **The Chronological Seal:** The Spirit's call to the churches (G4151) culminates in an equation that sums to the Hebrew year **5730 AM (1969 CE)**, the presenter's date of birth, sealing the testimony with a personal chronological marker.
