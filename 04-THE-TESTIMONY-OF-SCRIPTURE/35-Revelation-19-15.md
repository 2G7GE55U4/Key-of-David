# Revelation 19:15 – The Sharp Sword and the Rod of Iron

> *"And out of his mouth goeth a sharp sword, that with it he should smite the nations: and he shall rule them with a rod of iron: and he treadeth the winepress of the fierceness and wrath of Almighty God."* — Revelation 19:15 (KJV)

## The Prophetic Claim
This verse presents Christ in His triumphant return with **two instruments of judgment**:

1. **A Sharp Sword from His Mouth** – The word of God as a weapon of judgment (Hebrews 4:12, Ephesians 6:17).
2. **A Rod of Iron** – Absolute, unbreakable rule over the nations (Psalm 2:9).

This is the **executive judgment** phase of Christ's ministry: the Word made flesh now wields the Word as a sword to execute justice. The verse bridges **prophetic declaration** (sword from mouth) with **kingly authority** (rod of iron).
