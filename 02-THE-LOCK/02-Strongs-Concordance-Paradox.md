The Strong's Exhaustive Concordance was compiled by **James Strong** in 1890—centuries after the Bible was written. At first glance, its numbering system (H1-H8674 for Hebrew, G1-G5523 for Greek) appears to be a **human organizational tool**.

Yet the mathematics reveal something extraordinary: **The Bible appears to have been written with knowledge of these future numbers.**

This is a **temporal paradox** that points to one conclusion: **Divine authorship transcends time.**
